Imgur Tweaks
============================

This Google Chrome extension automatically extends functionality on the Imgur site.

When browsing through the gallery captions are initially hidden to prevent spoilers or people just being dicks.

The fairies appear on a user page, allowing you to upvote or downvote every single comment that user has ever made. Ever.

It'll have some other features, things people request, and stuff I make up just to annoy... I mean entertain... you.
